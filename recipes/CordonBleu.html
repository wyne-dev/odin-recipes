How to Make Cordon bleu

INGREDIENTS

    Main:

  • 4 chicken breasts (boneless, skinless)
  • 4 slices ham
  • 4 slices Swiss cheese

    Breading:

  • 1 cup flour
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 1Â½ cups breadcrumbs
  • Salt, pepper, paprika

    For cooking:

  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 2 tbsp oil

STEPS

  1. Pound chicken thin
  2. Add ham & cheese, roll up
  3. Coat in flour, egg, then breadcrumbs
  4. Secure with toothpicks
  5. Fry in butter & oil until golden (5-6 min per side)
